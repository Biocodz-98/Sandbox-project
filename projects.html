Welcome

Colors are visual perceptions created by light interacting with the eyes receptors. They are typically categorized into primary (red, blue, yellow), secondary (green, orange, purple), and tertiary hues. Colors can evoke emotions and have cultural or psychological associations: for example, red often represents passion or danger, while blue symbolizes calmness or stability. The color wheel helps understand color relationships, such as complementary colors (opposites on the wheel) that create contrast and harmony. Additionally, color perception is subjective, influenced by context, lighting, and individual differences.
